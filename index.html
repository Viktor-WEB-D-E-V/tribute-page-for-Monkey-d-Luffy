Monkey D. Luffy

The man who will become the future king of pirates

Here's a time line of major events

  • Birth: Born in Foosha Village in the East Blue. Raised by Curly Dadan and looked up to "Red-Haired" Shanks.

  • Childhood (Before Age 7): Eats the Gomu Gomu no Mi, gaining rubber powers but losing his ability to swim.

  • Age 7: Forms a brotherly bond with Ace and Sabo, dreaming of becoming the Pirate King.

  • Age 17: Sets sail alone to find the legendary One Piece. Forms the Straw Hat Pirates.

  • First Crew Members: Zoro, Nami, Usopp, and Sanji join his crew.

  • Enters Grand Line: Encounters Baroque Works and defeats Crocodile, a Warlord of the Sea.

  • Sky Island & Water 7: Fights Enel and later CP9 to save Robin, unlocking Gear Second & Gear Third.

  • Marineford War (Age 19): Fails to save Ace, who dies protecting him. This event pushes Luffy to train for two years.

  • Post-Timeskip (Age 19-20): Returns stronger, takes on new threats like Doflamingo, Big Mom, and Kaido. Unlocks Gear Fourth and later Gear Fifth (Nika Form).

  • Defeats Kaido: Liberates Wano, making him a major figure in the world.

  • Final Saga: Sets his sights on Laugh Tale, aiming to become the Pirate King.
